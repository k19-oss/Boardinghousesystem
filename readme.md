 System Description:
    The Boarding House Payment System is a specialized web-based digital solution designed to address the specific needs of boarding house operations catering to the academic community of CHMSU Binalbagan Campus. Recognizing the challenges faced by boarding house owners in managing finances and the need for students, faculty, and staff to have a clear view of their rental obligations, this system serves as a centralized platform to streamline and organize rental payment processes.

     It allows users—both administrators and tenants—to efficiently record payments, monitor due dates, maintain accurate tenant information, and generate comprehensive payment reports. By moving away from traditional, manual methods like pen-and-paper ledgers or basic spreadsheets, the system aims to significantly reduce common human errors, enhance transaction transparency, and make the entire payment process faster, more reliable, and convenient for everyone involved.
